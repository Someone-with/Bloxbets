How to Earn Gems and Convert to Points

In GemHaven, you can earn gems in two main ways, which can then be converted to points (100 gems = 1 point). These points can be deposited or donated via our system.

Buy Gems in the In-Game Store

Purchase gems directly from the in-game store using Robux. These gems can immediately be used for deposits. Support the game and get gems fast!

AFK for Passive Earnings

Stay in the game and AFK (Away From Keyboard) to earn 1 gem every minute. It's a simple way to accumulate gems over time without active play.

How to Withdraw and Deposit Points

Once you've collected enough gems, follow these steps to withdraw them to points via our bot:

  • Gather at least 100 gems (since 100 gems = 1 point).
  • In the game, hit the "Withdraw to Bot" button.
  • Enter the amount of gems you want to withdraw.
  • Provide your Discord User ID (UID) for verification and deposit.
  • Confirm, and your points will be deposited/donated accordingly!

Note: Ensure your Discord UID is correct to avoid any issues.
